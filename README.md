# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard Analysis

## 🔍 Problem Statement

This project analyzes sales performance, profitability, and customer trends using the Superstore dataset to generate actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning
* Data Visualization

## 📈 Key Metrics

* Total Sales: ₹22,97,201
* Total Profit: ₹2,86,397
* Profit Margin: 12%
* YoY Growth: 20.36%

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 💰 Business Performance

* The business is profitable, but operates at a moderate margin of 12%, indicating scope for margin optimization.

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

* The West region is the top-performing region in terms of both sales and profit.
* The South region underperforms and requires strategic improvement.

### 📦 Category Performance

* The Technology category is the primary revenue and profit driver.
* The Furniture category shows relatively low profitability, indicating inefficiencies.

### 📉 Loss Analysis

* Sub-categories such as Tables, Bookcases, and Supplies consistently generate losses, negatively impacting overall profitability.

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Customer Segment

* The Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales, making it the key target segment.

### 🏙 Top Locations

* New York City generates the highest sales, followed by other major urban centers.

### 💸 Discount Impact

* Higher discount levels are directly associated with reduced profitability, indicating inefficient pricing strategies.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategies to protect profit margins
* Re-evaluate or improve loss-making sub-categories
* Focus on expanding high-performing categories like Technology
* Strengthen business strategies in underperforming regions (South)
* Leverage high-performing cities for targeted growth initiatives
